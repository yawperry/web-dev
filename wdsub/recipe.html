Easy pizza recipe from home

    Ingredients

  1. Dough

    • 1 packet of active dry yeast(or baking powder for those with an allergy)
    • 1 teaspoon of sugar
    • 1 1/2 tablespoons of olive oil
    • 1 teaspoon of salt
    • 2 cups of bread flour(or all purpose)

  2. sauce

    • crushed tomatoes
    • sauteed 1 clove of minced garlic
    • dried oragano
    • dried basil
    • sugar

  3. cheese

    • milk(let it sit for a couple days)
    • garlic
    • old bay

  4. toppings

    • pepperoni
    • anchovies
    • jolly ranchers

  5. Enjoy
